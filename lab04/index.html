Cool 4X/Strategy Games

This website will be about 4X and Strategy games. I have played a lot of these types of games, and it's one of my favorite genres of games. Although I have played a lot of 4X and Strategy games, I have not played all of them. This website will not be limited to just the ones I have played.

What is a 4X Game

The four X's in 4X are explore, expand, exploit, and exterminate. So any game that contains the four X's could be considered a 4X game.

  • Civilization 5
